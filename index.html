About Us

Started in 2011, RCL Express is a prominent figure in the expediting business. With over 40 straight trucks, cargo vans, sprinter and semi trucks, we have the capacity to fill any lane that you might need! Our team of dispatchers work night and day to make sure your freight gets to its destination ontime, every time. From auto parts and machinery to frozen foods and furniture, we run it all. With us, you can have the peace of mind that your critical freight will be delived intact and ontime. Call us today and put your freight in good hands!
